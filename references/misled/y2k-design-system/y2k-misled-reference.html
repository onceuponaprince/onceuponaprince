> THE THESIS // 01

Misled is more than a brand. It is a refusal.

Young people in underrepresented communities are bombarded with pressures, algorithmic expectations, and false ideals that pull them away from who they actually are. They are misled — into chasing trends, into performing selves they didn't choose.

Most skate and streetwear brands in London don't stand for the people wearing them. They sell to the herd. We don't.

Our apparel and skateboards aren't just products. They are symbols of resistance against conformity.

Every deck we craft and every hoodie we produce holds the same line: a positive, supportive, authentic community of young people who proudly stand against the pressures that seek to mislead them.
